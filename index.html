🔐 Cybersecurity Tips & Best Practices

  • ✅ Use Strong Passwords: A mix of uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols (e.g., `P@ssw0rd!2025`).
  • ✅ Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA): Adds an extra layer of security to your accounts.
  • ✅ Beware of Phishing Emails: Don’t click suspicious links or download unknown attachments.
  • ✅ Update Your Software Regularly: Security patches fix vulnerabilities.
  • ✅ Use a VPN on Public Wi-Fi: Prevent hackers from intercepting your data.
